Privacy Policy for Pang Cyber

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Pang Cyber is a free arcade game developed by mahued73. This privacy policy explains how we handle user data.

Data Collection

Pang Cyber does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not access contacts, photos, or device files
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Does not share any data with third parties

All game progress (high scores, unlocked stages, settings) is stored locally on your device using the browser's localStorage API. This data never leaves your device.

Internet Permissions

The app requests INTERNET permission solely for future ad functionality (Google AdMob). No data is transmitted until ads are enabled.

Third-Party Services

Once AdMob is integrated, Google AdMob may collect anonymous usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads via your device's advertising settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as features are added. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact mahued73 via GitHub Issues.
